How it works
Three steps from procedure to verified record
1

Log the procedure

Type the procedure name — ICD-10 and CPT codes assign automatically. Approach, anaesthesia type, and duration pre-fill. The whole entry takes under two minutes.

2

Request counter-signature

Enter your supervising consultant's email. They receive a one-click verification link — no account required. When they confirm, the entry is marked verified.

3

Share your portfolio

Your public portfolio page lists every verified procedure with codes, approach, and institution. Send one link to any hospital, board, or fellowship program.

Built for surgeons who move

Locum work, international licensing, fellowship applications — your record follows you. Institutions trust verified entries because the counter-signature is tied to the exact procedure details.
